Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Pumpkin Pie

Whether you're hosting a festive party or a casual get-together with friends, our Famous Pumpkin Pie will make entertaining easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pie Crust
  • 3/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cloves
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 can of pumpkin puree
  • 1 can of evaporated milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Combine the dry ingreadients in a small bowl
  3. Whisk the eggs in a large bowl
  4. Add dry ingredients to the large bowl along with the pumpkin
  5. Slowly pour in milk
  6. Pour the mixture into the pie shell
  7. Bake for 15 minutes
  8. Reduce the temp of the oen to 350 degrees F and bake for additional 40-50 minutes
  9. Cool for 2 hours
